IGAP, the Independent Group of Analytical Psychologists, offers a programme of preparation to become a Jungian Analyst and a series of seminars open to the public, referrals for Jungian Analysis and low cost analysis. Members of IGAP are UKCP registered and members of the worldwide community of Jungian Analysts through International Association for Analytical Psychology.
